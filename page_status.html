14 Page status

Some pages in this online Catalog may be marked with various status badges. Please see below for details:

14.1 Under Review

Indicates that the contents or underlying data of a page are actively being reviewed. Please treat information on “Under Review” pages with care. For advice on using the information of a particular page, please email the SOE team (see below).

14.2 In Development

Indicates that a page and its contents are actively being worked on. The information on “In Development” pages is likely to change frequently and with little notice. Please revisit the page often to ensure you are using the latest contents and underlying data. For updates on the development of a particular page, please email the SOE team (see below).

14.3 Under Construction

Indicates that the structure or visualizations of a page are likely to change. Although the page itself may change, the contents and underlying data of “Under Construction” pages will likely stay the same and may be safely used.

If you have any questions about the status of a particular page or use of its underlying data, please email the SOE team (nefsc.soe.leads@noaa.gov) for assistance.
